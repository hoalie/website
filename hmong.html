ABOUT HMONG

Hmong people are an ethnic group from south east Asia.

There are three different Hmong Dialects:

  • There is Hmong White "hmoob dawb".
  • There is Hmong Green "hmoob ntsuab".
  • Standard CHinese Miao "dananshan"

Hmong people are nomads and does not have a land to call their own. Most of our history has been lost.

Triditionally Hmong people follow the religon Shamanism.
